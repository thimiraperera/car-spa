Ordering

Ordering from us is simple. You can order in two ways:

  • Message us directly on WhatsApp at 074 2 388 588 with the products you want.
  • Use the cart on our website. When you check out, the cart sends your order to us through WhatsApp.

Your order is confirmed once we reply on WhatsApp and agree on the items, the total and the delivery details. If a product is out of stock, we will let you know and suggest an alternative or a refund of any payment made.

We deliver island-wide across Sri Lanka. Delivery usually takes 2 to 4 working days, and Colombo and suburbs usually receive orders the next day.

Payments & security

We keep payment simple and safe. You can pay in two ways:

  • Cash on delivery: pay the courier in cash when your order arrives.
  • Bank transfer: we will share our bank details on WhatsApp, and we dispatch your order once the transfer is confirmed.

Here is the important part: our website never asks for or collects card details. There is no card payment form on carspa.lk. If you ever see a page claiming to be Car Spa LK asking for your card number, PIN or online banking password, do not enter anything and please report it to us right away.

All payment amounts are in Sri Lankan Rupees (LKR). Always confirm the total with us on WhatsApp before making a bank transfer.

Product safety

Our products are made for vehicle care and work best, and safest, when used correctly. Please:

  • Read and follow the instructions on the product label before use.
  • Keep all products away from children and pets.
  • Take extra care with solvent-based products such as degreasers and certain sprays. Use them in a well-ventilated area, away from open flames, and avoid contact with skin and eyes.
  • Store products in a cool, dry place with the cap closed tightly.

If a product gets into someone's eyes or is swallowed, follow the first aid guidance on the label and get medical help without delay.
